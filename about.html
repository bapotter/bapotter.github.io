About Me

I'm Brandon Allen Potter


Let me tell you a little about myself.

I am only 19 years old, but I have had plenty of experiences with all things web. First off, my father works for a technical company and because of this he introduced me to computers when I was very young. I can't remmeber a time when I wasn't on a computer playing mien sweeper or some form of a learning game. Those early moments in my life helped kickstart my drive into web development.

At the very beginning of my highschool years I was very lucky to have been accepted into a program for students interested in going to college after highschool, and this program was known as PTECH. They introduced me to HTML and basic CSS. Even though it was all beginner level I enjoyed it. Even if I wasn't very good at it to begin with. Skip to my senior year of highschool and I have learned Python, Java, Javascript, SQL, C#, C++, and Node.JS. This was all thanks to me having luck.
